FAQ

How safe is it?

The game is based on the smart contract of the Tron Network blockchain, it is completely decentralized and autonomous. All operations of participants with a contract are absolutely transparent. Project administrators do not have access to funds and contract data. The source code of the smart contract is open, and everyone can read it. Source code, Audit.

How to start earning?

In order to start making profits you need to purchase one or several types of transport for in-game currency (tickets), the profit from a working transport is automatically generated every hour. All income from transport is divided equally into Budget and Profit. The budget can be spent on the acquisition of new types of transport to expand business and increase profits. Profit can be withdrawn at any time, it can also be spent on the development of your business. Each type of transport in the game has a limited durability, such a system ensures prosperity for each tycoon in the game and a long project life.

How to play from a computer?

You need to install the TronLink or TronPay extension for the browser. After installation, you can create a new TRX wallet or import an existing TRX wallet, and then transfer TRX from the exchange to the wallet. Finally, log in to TronLink or TronPay, go to the site and play!

How to play from the phone?

You need to download TronLink, TronWallet or Math Wallet to your phone. After installation, you can create a new TRX wallet or import an existing TRX wallet, and then transfer TRX from the exchange or another wallet to your wallet on your mobile device. Finally, find the TronEmpire in the wallet app or navigate to our site using the in-app browser and proceed to the game.

What is your commission size?

The administration fee is 10% of the amount spent on the purchase of transport. It is already included in the cost of transport, there will be no additional costs. These funds will be used for the long-term development of the project.

How does the referral system work?

You get 5% of all charges to your friend. Both from those that he credits himself buying tickets and from those that come to him as a reward from friends. That is, if a friend of your friend bought 10,000 tickets, then a friend got 500 tickets, and you get 5% of those 500 - 25 tickets.

Can't find the answer to your question?

If you do not find the answer to your question, you can always ask it in the official Telegram chat administration or project participants will gladly help you!
